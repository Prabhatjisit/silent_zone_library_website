# Guide: Generating Promo Videos using Image-to-Video AI

This guide shows you how to use **TopMediai** or **Tail Frame** (Image-to-Video models) to convert your static app screenshots into dynamic, cinematic promotional video clips.

---

## 🚀 Steps to Generate Video Clips

1. Open your Image-to-Video AI tool (e.g. **TopMediai** / **Tail Frame**).
2. Upload the corresponding screenshot image as the **Image Input / Source**.
3. Copy and paste the specialized prompt below into the **Motion Prompt / Text Prompt** field.
4. Set the camera motion parameters (if available) to **Slow Zoom In** or **Pan Right** for a cinematic look.
5. Generate the video!

---

## 📝 Recommended Prompts for each Screenshot

### 1. Student Dashboard (`screenshot_1.png`)
* **Visual Content**: Student Home page with entry timer.
* **AI Video Prompt**:
  > *"A premium smartphone floating in a futuristic dark focus room environment, showcasing a glowing mobile interface with numbers counting up smoothly. Soft cinematic purple studio lighting, slow camera pan, high resolution, 3D rotating device."*

### 2. Admin Seats Grid (`screenshot_2.png`)
* **Visual Content**: The 40-seat batch occupancy map.
* **AI Video Prompt**:
  > *"Close up shot of a smartphone screen showing a colorful 40-seat seating grid map. Seating slots dynamically highlight, rotating in 3D space, tech-neon glow background, high-fidelity 4K render, smooth camera rotation."*

### 3. Attendance History Logs (`screenshot_3.png`)
* **Visual Content**: Student attendance duration records.
* **AI Video Prompt**:
  > *"Close up of a mobile app screen slowly scrolling down a list of glassmorphic log rows. Text and badges glow softly, clean depth of field background, high tech aesthetic, smooth scroll."*

### 4. Admin Student Profile (`screenshot_4.png`)
* **Visual Content**: Subscription cards & "Allot Seat" action.
* **AI Video Prompt**:
  > *"A close up shot of a clean thumb tapping a green 'Allot Seat' button on a mobile phone interface. Subtle futuristic ripple and particle animation expands from the tap, premium studio lighting."*

### 5. Admin Dashboard
* **Visual Content**: Admin Main Dashboard (active student lists & occupancy metrics).
* **AI Video Prompt**:
  > *"A modern smartphone screen displaying a real-time library analytics dashboard with glowing charts and metrics. The camera slowly zooms in as numbers and status badges update dynamically. Premium glassmorphic interface, dark mode, high tech focus room background."*

---

## 🎬 How to Assemble the Final Ad Video
1. Generate the **4 video clips** (each will be around 4–5 seconds long).
2. Download them and import them into **CapCut** or **Canva**.
3. Place them in order:
   * **Clip 1**: Student Dashboard (Hook)
   * **Clip 2**: Seats Grid Map (Features)
   * **Clip 3**: Attendance Scroll (Detail)
   * **Clip 4**: Admin Allot Seat Tap (Action)
4. Add a voiceover and modern upbeat background music.
5. Export as `walkthrough.mp4` and upload it to `/assets/videos/walkthrough.mp4` on your website!
